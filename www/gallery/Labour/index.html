Labour

BACK

Labour is a series of photos documenting my time spent working on construction. The series was captured in a rather unconventional context, as my days consisted entirely of labour, which left little time for photography. The photos were made in the short periods of down time partitioning the seemingly unending stream of labour that comprises a day on construction. The images are split into three separate triptychs, with each row exploring a unique visual style. All the photos were captured on and edited with an iPhone 5.
