About

My name is Jared Krause, I'm a street and travel photographer from Canada. I love photography, capturing candid moments, viewing the beautiful photography Toronto gallerys, and collecting an increasingly large amount of photography books.

I usually use an Olympus OMD EM-5 with a few prime lenses, and a Sony RX100 as backup. I also use my iPhone 5 to take photos for Instagram.

I am also web developer currently working in Toronto. For inquiries feel free to contact me or connect with me on github.
